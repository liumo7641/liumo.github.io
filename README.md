## About Me & Research Interests

My research lies at Microeconomic Theory and Policy Economics. I am driven by a desire to not only evaluate if a policy works empirically but to understand why it is designed that way and how its theoretical properties drive outcomes.

**Primary Research Interests:**
* **Mechanism Design & Matching Theory:** Stability, strategy-proofness, and market design.
* **Applied Microeconomic Theory:** Auction theory and public policy modeling.
* **Economics of Education:** Teacher evaluation metrics and school choice systems.
